# 08 — Client Portal

**Faith Counseling User Manual**

---

## Overview

The Client Portal is the secure, client-facing side of Faith Counseling. Clients use it to manage their profile, complete intake forms, view assigned documents, request appointments, see their counselor's contact info, and exercise their data rights.

---

## 8.1 Accessing the Portal

Clients receive a portal invitation from their practice. The invitation includes:

- The portal URL
- Login credentials (or a link to create a password)

Once logged in, the client sees their personalized portal dashboard.

---

## 8.2 Portal Dashboard

The portal dashboard is the client's home screen. It shows:

- **Welcome message** from the practice
- **Pending actions** — forms or documents awaiting completion
- **Upcoming appointments** — confirmed and pending appointment requests
- **Counselor info** — a brief card showing the assigned counselor

---

## 8.3 Profile Tab

The Profile tab allows clients to view and update their personal information.

### What Clients Can Edit

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Name** | First and last name |
| **Contact information** | Phone, email, address |
| **Date of birth** | Use `MM/DD/YYYY` format |
| **Emergency contact** | Name and phone number |
| **Employment status** | Current employment situation |
| **Education level** | Highest completed education level |
| **Preferred language** | Primary communication language |

Click **Save** after making changes.

---

## 8.4 Appointments Tab

Clients can request appointments through the portal.

### Viewing Appointments

- **Confirmed appointments** — scheduled sessions with date, time, and location (in-person or telehealth)
- **Pending requests** — appointment requests that are awaiting staff confirmation

### Requesting an Appointment

1. Click **Request Appointment**.
2. Select the requested service type.
3. Provide preferred dates and times.
4. Add any notes for the practice.
5. Submit.

Practice staff will review the request and confirm or contact the client directly.

---

## 8.5 Documents Tab

The Documents tab is where clients complete assigned intake forms and upload supporting documents.

### Assigned Forms and Packets

When a counselor or admin assigns a form, it appears here with:

- Form name and description
- Assigned date
- Due date (if set)
- Status: Assigned, In Progress, Submitted

### Completing a Form

1. Click the form name.
2. The form runner opens with all fields.
3. Complete all required fields.
4. Click **Submit**.

Submitted forms are immediately visible to practice staff.

### Uploading Documents

Clients can also upload supporting documents (insurance cards, referral letters, etc.):

1. Click **Upload Document**.
2. Select the file from your device.
3. Add a description.
4. Click **Upload**.

---

## 8.6 Counselor Tab

The Counselor tab shows information about the client's assigned counselor:

- Counselor name and photo (if available)
- License type and number
- Bio and specialty areas
- Contact method (typically handled through the practice, not direct email)

---

## 8.7 Financials / Giving Tab

The Giving/Financials tab provides a view of financial arrangements and statements relevant to the client's care.

This tab may include:

- Current service arrangement (standard fee, sliding scale, pro bono, insurance)
- Outstanding balance information (if exposed by the practice)
- Offering history

The content of this tab depends on what the practice has configured.

---

## 8.8 Resources Tab

The Resources tab provides links and materials provided by the practice, such as:

- Ministry resources
- Educational materials
- Crisis line contact information
- Faith-based reading materials

This content is curated by the practice administrator.

---

## 8.9 Data Rights Tab

Faith Counseling includes a **Data Rights** feature aligned with privacy best practices.

### What Clients Can Do

| Request Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Access** | Request a copy of all data the practice holds |
| **Correction** | Request a correction to inaccurate records |
| **Deletion** | Request deletion of data (subject to legal and retention requirements) |
| **Restriction** | Request that certain data not be processed |
| **Portability** | Request data in a portable format |

### Submitting a Data Rights Request

1. Click **Data Rights** in the portal navigation.
2. Select the request type.
3. Describe the specific request.
4. Submit.

Practice staff will receive the request and respond within the legally required timeframe.

---

## 8.10 Changing Your Password

To change your portal password:

1. Navigate to profile settings or look for the **Change Password** option in the portal account panel.
2. Enter your current password.
3. Enter and confirm the new password.
4. Click **Save**.

Use a strong, unique password. Contact the practice if you are locked out.

---

## 8.11 Logging Out

Click **Sign Out** in the top bar to securely end your session. Sessions also expire after a period of inactivity.
