# 09 — Forms and Documents

**Faith Counseling User Manual**

---

## Overview

Faith Counseling includes a full-featured forms and document management system. Practice staff can assign clinical and intake forms to clients, manage submissions, and track completion status. Clients complete forms through the [Client Portal](08-client-portal.md).

---

## 9.1 Form Library

The form library contains all available forms and intake packets for the practice. Forms include:

- Intake and onboarding packets
- Clinical assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, and others)
- Consent forms
- Treatment agreements
- Faith integration preference surveys
- Safety screenings
- Custom practice forms

> **Platform Admin / Practice Owner:** Contact your implementation team to add custom forms to the library.

---

## 9.2 Assigning Forms to Clients

Forms can be assigned from two places:

### From Workspace Studio — Documents Tab

1. Open **Workspace Studio** → **Documents**.
2. Select the client from the client picker.
3. Browse the form library and select the form(s) to assign.
4. Optionally set a **due date**.
5. Click **Assign**.

### From Client Detail — Documents Shortcut

1. Open the client record.
2. Click the **Documents** quick action in the header.
3. The Documents tab opens with this client pre-selected.
4. Follow the same assignment steps.

---

## 9.3 Intake Packets

Intake packets are bundles of forms grouped for the onboarding workflow. A single intake packet may include:

- Demographics and contact form
- Insurance information form
- Consent and disclosure agreement
- Faith preference survey
- Initial screening questionnaire

### Assigning an Intake Packet

Assign an intake packet the same way as a single form. The client will see all forms in the packet listed in their portal Documents tab with a completion progress indicator.

---

## 9.4 Tracking Form Status

### Staff View

From the Documents tab in Workspace Studio, each client's assignment history shows:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Assigned** | The form has been sent to the client |
| **In Progress** | The client has opened and started the form |
| **Submitted** | The client has submitted the completed form |
| **Reviewed** | A staff member has reviewed the submission |

### Reviewing a Submission

1. Open the Documents tab.
2. Find the submitted form.
3. Click **View Submission** to read the client's responses.
4. Mark as **Reviewed** when complete.

---

## 9.5 Intake Preview Tab

From the Client Detail page, the **Intake Preview** tab provides a read-only structured view of the client's completed intake responses. This brings intake information directly into the clinical workflow without requiring staff to open the portal documents area separately.

---

## 9.6 Document Uploads

Clients can upload supporting documents through the portal (insurance cards, referral letters, etc.). Uploaded files are visible to staff in:

- The Client Portal Documents view (staff-side)
- The Workspace Studio Documents tab

### Reviewing Uploaded Files

1. Open the Downloads/Uploads section within the client's document list.
2. Click the file name to download and review.
3. Add review notes if needed.

---

## 9.7 Document and Form Best Practices

| Practice | Recommendation |
|---|---|
| Assign forms at intake | Send the intake packet as soon as the client record is created |
| Set due dates | Due dates help clients complete forms before their first session |
| Review submissions promptly | Clinical assessments should be reviewed before the next session |
| Use the Intake Preview tab | Review intake answers without navigating away from the clinical workspace |
