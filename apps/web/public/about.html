About Faith Counseling Workspace

Run the practice from one intentional workspace.

Operational console for counseling, documentation, billing, portal, and reporting workflows. This workspace centralizes day-to-day operations for faith-integrated counseling practices with role-aware views, clinical workflows, scheduling, client portal interactions, and billing/reporting workflows backed by API endpoints.

Role-aware navigation Clinical and faith workflows API-backed operations Built-in health and telemetry

What this app does

Brings the operational rhythm of a counseling practice into one place: charting, appointments, claims, portal exchange, and reporting, all with direct access to the platform’s health and documentation surfaces.

4 core workflow families
3 documentation and monitoring entry points
1 shared operational console

Core Modules

The day-to-day operating surface.

The workspace is organized around the practical flow of a counseling practice, from intake and charting to follow-up scheduling, billing, and faith-integrated support workflows.

CL

Client Lifecycle & Charting

Client records, treatment plans, progress notes, consents, intake packets.

SO

Scheduling & Operations

Appointments, reminders, waitlist, staffing, and availability support.

BC

Billing & Claims

Service codes, fee schedules, invoices, payments, superbills, claims, aging.

PF

Portal & Faith Workflows

Portal messaging/documents plus faith resources, goals, and inventory workflows.

Platform & Docs

Health, contracts, and live operator visibility.

Monitoring
